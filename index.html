Sepsis Survival Prediction

In this project, I built a stacked ensemble model to predict the 30-day mortality risk for sepsis patients. Using SHAP, I explain model predictions at both the global and individual level, providing actionable insights for clinicians. Click **Dashboard** to interact with the live model and see how the predictions and explanations change with patient features.
